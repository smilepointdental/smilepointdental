About Us

Smilepoint Multispeciality Dental Clinic

“Smiles start from heart before expressed out of mouth”

Throughout our history, we have been providing best oral care treatments to help our patients achieve and enjoy a lifetime of healthy and beautiful smiles.

We educate patients about their problems and discuss options and help the patients to choose appropriate treatment based on their individual needs and goals.

Our dentists will always have your best interest at heart and offer exceptional care for your complete and satisfactory smile.

We are proud to provide state-of-the-art facilities for the highest quality dental care available. It is one of our top priorities to protect the well-being of our valued patients. We are confident that you will feel right at home in our clinic as we welcome all patients as if they are a family.

We’re committed to forming relationships with our patients built on trust.

Message from Chief
Dr. Anand Sankar:

“As a holistic dentist and wellness expert, my approach to your health extends beyond dental care. I look forward to working with you to optimize your nutrition, diet, and mental and physical wellbeing. I help my patients become educated, empowered, and active participants in their whole body health.

At Smile point dental clinic, we’ve invested in the latest advancements in high-tech dentistry and enhance your visit with relaxing and pleasant experience.

It’s time for you to achieve healthy teeth and gums for life!”
